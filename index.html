Sebastian Linde

Assistant Professor of Economics

Grand Valley State University

Biography

Sebastian Linde is an assistant professor of economics at the department of economics at Grand Valley State University. His research is focused on the health care industry, and his interests include network formation, bargaining and pricing, mergers and acquisitions, and hospital cost efficiency.
